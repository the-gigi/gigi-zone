8. ## Take Home Points
- Configuration lives in three zones: home (~/.claude/), project (.claude/), and enterprise (system paths). Each has its own scope and audience.
- settings.json is the universal control panel, appearing at every scope with array settings merging across layers.
- Project config is for the team (committed to git). User config is for you (stays local). Keep them separate.
- Start with /init to bootstrap, then grow organically. Don't over-engineer upfront.
- Each piece was covered in depth across the series. This article is the map.
